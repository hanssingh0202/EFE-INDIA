About Us

EFE INDIA, is Make in India company engaged in the procurement, supply, outsourcing of exclusive and critical parts of Renewable sector-wind turbine blades.
With more than 7 years of experience in the wind sector and supported in manufacturing by one of the best experts in the wind industry for blade parts. EFE team's mission is to offer parts that will boost customer Localization initiatives for Indian sources to Indian demand with supreme quality, cost-effectiveness, and an improved supply chain.
EFE is working for Indigenous parts, Local manufacturing, Procurement, Lead time, leveraging domestic supply chain for wind turbine blades, and contributing towards India’s Energy Independence Target of 500 GW by 2030.
